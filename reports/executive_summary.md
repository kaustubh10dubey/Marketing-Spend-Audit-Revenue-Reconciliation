## üí° Recommendations

### Budget Reallocation Strategy
We recommend a strategic reallocation of **$300,000** from the Display and Social channels to SEO and SEM to maximize ROI.

- **Decrease üîª:**
    - Display: -$150,000 (75% reduction)
    - Social: -$150,000 (50% reduction)
- **Increase üî∫:**
    - SEO: +$150,000 (60% increase)
    - SEM: +$150,000 (37.5% increase)

- **Old Total Actual Revenue:** $2.2M
- **Projected Increase:** **~$2.5M**
- **New Projected ROI:** $4.695M / $1.4M = **3.35x**

This data-driven approach will align marketing spend with actual performance, driving significant growth and profitability.
